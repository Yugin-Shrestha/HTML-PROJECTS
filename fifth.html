Why Visit Nepal?

Nepal offers breathtaking natural beauty, rich culture, and adventure activities. From Mount Everest to serene lakes, it’s a traveler's paradise.

Highlights

  • Trekking in Annapurna & Langtang
  • Wildlife safaris in Chitwan
  • Cultural tours of Kathmandu Valley
  • Adventure sports like rafting & paragliding
  • Spiritual experiences in Lumbini

Top Destinations

Kathmandu

Capital city with UNESCO World Heritage Sites like Durbar Square and Swayambhunath. Gateway to Himalayan treks.

Pokhara

City of Lakes with Phewa Lake, paragliding, and Annapurna Base Camp treks.

Chitwan

National park with wildlife safaris: tigers, rhinos, and birds. Cultural experiences with Tharu community.

Lumbini

Birthplace of Lord Buddha with monasteries and the Mayadevi Temple. Ideal for spiritual reflection.
